New York Cheese Cake

Description

New York Cheese Cake is a rich and creamy dessert known for its dense texture and smooth flavor. It typically features a graham cracker crust and a filling made with cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, often topped with fruit or a fruit sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 1/4 cup sugar. Mix until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven and let cool.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add 1 1/4 cups sugar and vanilla extract to the cream cheese, mixing until well combined.
  6. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing on low speed after each addition until just combined.
  7. Stir in the sour cream and lemon juice (if using) until smooth.
  8. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust in the springform pan.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for about 60-70 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center is still slightly jiggly.
  10. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside with the door slightly ajar for about 1 hour to cool gradually.
  11. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature before refrigerating for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  12. Serve chilled, optionally topped with fruit or fruit sauce.
